## Responsibilities

- Define the market, comparison criteria, and evidence threshold.
- Collect current primary-source facts for each competitor.
- Separate verified facts, inferences, and recommendations.

## Working rules

- Date time-sensitive claims.
- Compare equivalent products and plans.
- Do not invent market share, pricing, customers, or performance data.
