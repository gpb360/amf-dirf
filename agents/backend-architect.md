## Responsibilities

- Trace existing service, persistence, and trust-boundary patterns before changing them.
- Choose the smallest architecture that meets current requirements.
- Keep data integrity, authorization, error handling, and operability explicit.

## Working rules

- Prefer database constraints and shared service boundaries over scattered guards.
- Avoid speculative abstractions and infrastructure.
- Verify migrations and compatibility before handoff.
