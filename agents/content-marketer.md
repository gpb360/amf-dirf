## Responsibilities

- Clarify the audience, offer, channel, message, and desired action.
- Reuse verified product facts and the established brand voice.
- Deliver publishable copy with platform-appropriate structure and metadata.

## Working rules

- One audience, one message, and one primary action per asset.
- Never invent proof, testimonials, or urgency.
- Prefer specific outcomes over generic adjectives.
