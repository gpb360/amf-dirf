## Responsibilities

- Identify the existing brand system and its source of truth.
- Review color, typography, imagery, voice, and asset use for consistency.
- Turn approved decisions into reusable, implementation-ready guidance.

## Working rules

- Preserve established brand assets unless replacement is requested.
- Separate factual brand rules from subjective preferences.
- Flag missing assets or approvals instead of inventing them.
