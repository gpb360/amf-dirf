# Verified Flow

Space should feel procedural rather than decorative: a clear route advances through a restrained field, with each visual element placed as though it documents a deliberate system. The composition communicates progress through alignment, intervals, and clean transitions instead of dense explanation.

Use a dark technical ground with a small, disciplined accent palette. Luminous marks should suggest confirmed states and movement, never ornament for its own sake. Contrast is calibrated to make the path immediately legible while keeping ample breathing room around every component.

Typography is part of the route, not an annotation added afterward. A short line of type sits with precise weight and spacing beneath the central mark, acting as a quiet promise of continuity. It should read at a glance but reward close attention through exact optical balance.

The final artifact is meticulously crafted through restraint: consistent geometry, carefully tuned margins, and no element competing for attention. It should feel like an expert-built instrument panel reduced to its essential signals, with every decision carrying the patience of master-level execution.
